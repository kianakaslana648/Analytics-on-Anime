Data Gathering

The datasets I've gathered are mainly from:

•MyAnimeList, the biggest anime website which provides the largest dataset in the world about information of animes;

•Bangumi, a website which provides the largest dataset in China about information of animes;

•Bilibili, the most popular anime app in China, which provides anime programmes online and contains lots of 'Bullet Screens';

•Twitter, the most popular microblogging and social networking service in the world;

Surely I will collect data from more sources if needed. I'm still trying to get the permission of APIs on other websites.

Here are the links of datasets I've collected:

First1000PopAnime.txt

It collects [Name, Score, Link] of Top 1000 Popular animes from MyAnimeList.

First1000RatingAnime.txt

It collects [Name, Score, Link] of Top 1000 Rank animes from MyAnimeList.

1000POPInfo.txt

It collects [Name, Link, Type, Status, Aired Time, Season, Broadcast Time, Producers, Licensors, Studios, Source, Tags, Duration, Rating, Score, Rank, Popularity, Members, Favorites] of Top 1000 Rank animes from MyAnimeList.

1000RKInfo.txt

It collects [Name, Link, Type, Status, Aired Time, Season, Broadcast Time, Producers, Licensors, Studios, Source, Tags, Duration, Rating, Score, Rank, Popularity, Members, Favorites] of Top 1000 Popular animes from MyAnimeList.

First1000RatingAnimeBangumi.txt

It collects [Name, Link] of Top 1000 Popular animes from Bangumi.

1000RKInfoBangumi.txt

It collects [Name, Score, Link, Tags] of Top 1000 Popular animes from Bangumi.

AnimebyYear

It collects [Name, Link] of animes of years from 2000 to 2021 from MyAnimeList.

bilibili bullet screens

It collects bullet screens of three animes from bilibili using the tool of biliplus.

tweets

It collects 3000 tweets on 3 animes from Twitter.

Here are the links of scripts I've used to scrape the data:

scrapeTopRank&TopPopMAL.py

It collects 'First1000PopAnime.txt' & 'First1000RatingAnime.txt'.

scrapeTagsMostRankMAL.py

It collects '1000RKInfo.txt'.

scrapeTagsMostPopMAL.py

It collects '1000POPInfo.txt'.

scrapeTopRankBangumi.py

It collects 'First1000RatingAnimeBangumi.txt'.

scrapeTagsTopRankBangumi.py

It collects '1000RKInfoBangumi.txt'.

scrapeAnimebyYearMAL.py

It collects all the data in 'AnimebyYear'.

api_collect_tweets_Naruto.py

api_collect_tweets_SteinsGate.py

api_collect_tweets_violet.py

Each of them collects 1000 tweets on one anime.

What needs to be mentioned is that during use, the codes need to be adjusted for the circle range and opening-format of files, because usually you cannot scrape all the data at one time--you will be restricted by the website sometimes and you need to continue to scrape the remaining data once validated.
