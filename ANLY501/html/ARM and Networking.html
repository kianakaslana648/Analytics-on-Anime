ARM


Basic Concepts

ARM is the abbreviation of Association Rule Mining. It aims at seeking for the rules in the transaction data. The rules suggest that we can assert that given the first element, the second element is much likely to exist simultaneously.(Measured by Conf and Lift) And the rules are not minor.(Measured by Sup)

In my examples the most strict restriction for generating at least 10 rules is about lower bound of 0.02-0.1 for Sup with lower bound of 0.8-1 for Conf.

Basic Explanation:

Top rules on the left are defined to be the top rules in the frequent sets in the order of some metric.

In this example, the first rule of TopSup is {animesx_} to {violet}, which means elements of the rule occurs simultaneously with relatively higher frequency (Lower bound of Sup) and the second element occurs with a high probability given the first element occurs. (Lower bound of Conf)

The first rule of TopConf is {means} to {violet}, which means its Conf comes the first in the rule set.

The first rule of TopLift is {know, want} to {means}, which means the two elements of it has close relation. (Relatively large value of Lift)

Comment:

In violet evergarden, the top rules in the frequent sets in three orders seem to be similar, with elements of words of love, want, violet, etc.

• In tweets on Violet Evergarden, there are associations among words of love, want, know, mean. That indicates people watching this anime tend to have deep and positive thoughts--that's what the anime conveys. These words are not minor because in tweets of many other animes, even no association can be found under such a strict standard.

Violet

• In tweets on Naruto, the most frequently occuring associations contain akatsuki, dawn. Those are different appellations of the same orgnization in the anime series, which was the biggest antagonist orgnization. People like the orgnization because the members are the kind of people with strong determination and cool appearance.

Akatsuki

• As for tweets on FGO, we should first mention that FGO is a famous game with anime characters. The collected tweets are primarily for the last 7 days. (Yeah the famous Italian classical composer) And book is a kind of resource in the game. That association indicates players are feeding the new character with resources. That could be some reference for the game developers for these several days.

Personal Opinions about ARM:

• In ARM method, the choices for the restrictions, lower bounds of Sup and Conf, are quite important.

• There are some difficulties for ARM method in the stage of Tweets:

(1) People always talk about different contents even within a certain topic. For example, when talking about a certain anime, they can talk about the plots, the cosplayers, some nice pictures, or even just a simple sentence saying that 'XXX' is so cool! The huge range of the contents cannot lead to some basic rules!

(2) While transaction data of transactions gives explicit restrictions to the elements in the baskets, transaction data of text is sometimes composed of different words with the same meaning. Although the method of lemmatization is available, you can still see in the example of Naruto, the two words of akatsuki and dawn still not belong to the same group--We cannot have a infinite lemmatization dictionary that deals with everything for us!
